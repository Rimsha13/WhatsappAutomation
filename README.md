# AI Marketing Strategy Automation

An AI-powered automation workflow built with **n8n**, **OpenAI**, and multiple integrations to automatically generate personalized marketing strategies for businesses and content creators.

This project transforms a manual consulting process into an intelligent AI-powered workflow that analyzes business information, generates strategic recommendations, and produces customized marketing plans within minutes.

---

# Overview

Marketing agencies and consultants often spend hours researching businesses, preparing audits, and creating detailed marketing strategies.

This workflow automates the entire process using AI, allowing users to generate high-quality business insights and personalized marketing plans with minimal manual effort.

---

# Business Problem

Creating customized marketing strategies traditionally requires:

- Manual business research
- Strategy planning
- Content planning
- Document preparation
- Client reporting

This process is repetitive, time-consuming, and difficult to scale.

---

# Solution

The automation workflow collects business information, processes it using AI, and automatically generates a complete marketing strategy tailored to the client's needs.

The system significantly reduces manual effort while maintaining high-quality outputs.
